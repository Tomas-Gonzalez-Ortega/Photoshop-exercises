About

Thomas A Anderson is the alias used by Tomas Gonzalez in Canada. An extremely personable individual with a passion for music, technology and sound. Highly creative, self motivated and eager to deliver quality products to his clients. Polite, reliable, punctual, knowledgeable but always willing to learn new things.

  • 2001-2007

    Humble Beginnings

    He started his path with a BA in Sound Engineering at AIEP in Andres Bello University, Santiago, Chile. He shines in areas like Midi and Postproduction. During 2003 he starts a freelance career as a Boom operator and music composer.

  • 2009-2013

    A musician is Born

    Then time came for more challenges, so he took a Postgraduate in Musical composition at the prestigious Universidad de Chile, to pull the business to the next level. He learned aspects of musical composition and its analysis through the study of instrumentation, orchestration, and other techniques such as tonality processes and electroacoustic music composition: a study of compositional techniques using technology (DSP) as an essential tool in music production (Sibelius, Pro Tools, MaxMsp).

  • 2010-2014

    Sound engineering Instructor

    He became a succesful instructor for sound engineering and sound technology students at the Institute of Technology IPCHILE. His subjects were Post production, Midi Instrument, Multimedia and Sound Equipment Laboratory. His students learned softwares like Pro Tools, Reason, Cubase and techniques such ADR and Foley. Respected by co-workers and students he performed at the top level in the institution designing lessons, encouraging students to learn and relating his industry experience with the daily-basis learning process.

  • 2014-Present

    Canada Expansion

    He settled in Vancouver to follow his dream to become a Sound developer. He's currently enrolled in Computer Science at Langara College and expects to achieve the characteristics that every audio developer should have: An Audio, Music and Programming background! His current experience includes freelance work specializing in animations, radio jingles, internet commercials and various audiovisual projects as a Sound Designer, Boom Operator, Audio Mixer, Audio Recorder and Musical Composer. He has a tremendous level of proficiency in DAW systems using Pro Tools and also strong post production skills in Foley, ADR, location recordings, synchro and mixing.
